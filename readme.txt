== Description ==

Easily schedule thousands of photos and posts to your social pages
We believe hitting all keys of social media efficiently and effectively is the key to coming on top of your competitors. Having a strong social media presence is extremely important to creating a lasting brand.

=  Scheduling made easy =
vBSocial Scheduler is the smart way to managing Social Media. No need to hire a social media manager. This plugin does it all for you. Grab 1000 photos related to your site. Zip them all in a folder and upload them using our plugin. Then schedule them at an interval to post every 10 minutes, 5 hours, 3 days etc.

= Increase Social Traffic to your Page and to your website =
Using an auto scheduler to auto post will drive more traffic to your page. Simply hashtag the text that gets posted to every image, and anyone viewing those hashtags will view your image, thus directing new traffic to your page and website. Its easy, it costs zero advertising dollars, and you’ll get likes for free.

= Get Started Quickly =
On install, the plugin works right out of the box.   

= Spend 1 hour, and let the plugin run for months =
Depending on how many images you upload to your server, and the interval you set, you can upload images with specific text attached to them, or hasha
